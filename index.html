About Me

Dr. Arif did his bachelor's degree from Dhaka, Bangladesh in 1992. He briefly worked in Zambia and then came to South Africa in October 1994. He grew up as a doctor in South Africa, working at different training hospitals,did his senior surgical rotationfrom groote schuur hospital,cape town and specializing in general surgery.He became a fellow of the Royal College of Surgeons in Dublin in 2003 and the South African College of Surgeons in 2005,he worked as a general surgeon since then and gained valuable surgical experience. He has been a specialist general surgeon at Melomed Private Hospital Group since January 2016.
